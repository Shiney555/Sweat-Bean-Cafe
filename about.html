About Sweet Bean Café

Welcome to Sweet Bean Café — your cozy neighborhood spot for handcrafted coffee, freshly baked treats, and warm smiles. We began with one dream — to create a space where friends gather, stories brew, and every sip feels like home.

Our beans are locally sourced, our pastries baked with love, and our team serves happiness with every cup. Whether you’re here for a latte break, a sweet indulgence, or just a moment to unwind, Sweet Bean Café is your happy place.
